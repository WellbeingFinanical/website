Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Wellbeing Financial and our AI-powered financial insights platform

Getting Started

How does Wellbeing Financial work?

Wellbeing Financial uses AI technology to relentlessly monitor the Canadian financial market and analyze your current financial products. Our AI evaluates mortgage rates, credit cards, personal loans, and other financial products 24/7, identifying opportunities where you could save money or get better terms. We present these insights through easy-to-understand opportunity scores and recommendations.

What makes Wellbeing Financial different from other financial apps?

We work exclusively for you, not the lenders. Our AI evaluates every financial product purely based on what's best for your situation, regardless of which institution offers it. To us, lenders are just different sources of capital, and we'll always recommend whoever offers you the best deal. We don't play favorites with any financial institution.

How do I get started with Wellbeing Financial?

Download the Wellbeing Financial app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Create your profile with basic information, then securely connect your financial accounts using our bank-level encrypted connection. Our AI will begin analyzing your situation immediately and provide personalized recommendations within hours.

How much does Wellbeing Financial cost?

The Wellbeing Financial app is free to use. When you choose to switch to a financial product we recommend, we may receive a referral fee from the mortgage broker, credit card provider, or lender. This never affects our recommendations—our only loyalty is to you, and we evaluate every product purely on its merit for your situation.

Privacy & Security

Is my financial data safe with Wellbeing Financial?

Yes. We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption and have read-only access to your accounts. We cannot move money, make purchases, or access your login credentials. Your data is stored in secure Canadian servers with multiple layers of protection. We're working toward SOC 2 Type II compliance and never sell your personal information.

What information do you access from my accounts?

We access account balances, transaction history, current rates, and terms for your existing financial products (mortgages, credit cards, loans). This allows our AI to understand your current situation and identify better opportunities. We only view this information—we cannot make changes, transfers, or payments.

Can I disconnect my accounts at any time?

Absolutely. You can disconnect any or all of your accounts at any time through the app settings. If you disconnect accounts, we'll no longer receive updates about those financial products, but you can reconnect them later if desired.

AI Insights & Recommendations

What are opportunity scores and how do they work?

Opportunity scores (1-100) help you quickly identify which financial products could benefit you most. Scores 90-100 indicate excellent opportunities with significant savings potential. 70-89 are good opportunities worth investigating. 50-69 show moderate benefits. Below 50 indicates limited benefit compared to your current situation. Our AI calculates these scores based on your current rates, credit profile, and market conditions.

How often are insights updated?

Our AI monitors the market continuously and updates insights as rates and terms change. You'll receive notifications when significant new opportunities arise or when market conditions change in ways that could benefit you.

How accurate are the savings estimates?

Our savings estimates are based on current market rates and your specific financial profile. While we strive for accuracy, final rates and terms depend on lender approval processes, credit checks, and other factors. We recommend using our estimates as a starting point for your financial decisions.

Using the Platform

What types of financial products does Wellbeing Financial analyze?

We analyze mortgages, personal loans, auto loans, lines of credit (HELOC), and credit cards across all major Canadian financial institutions. Our AI treats every lender as just another source of capital, evaluating products purely on what's best for your specific situation.

Can Wellbeing Financial help me apply for new financial products?

We provide insights and recommendations, and can connect you with licensed mortgage brokers and financial professionals who can help with applications. While our platform is educational, we work with qualified partners who can provide personalized advice and assistance with the application process.

Does Wellbeing Financial provide financial advice?

Wellbeing Financial provides educational technology and analysis for informational purposes only. We are not a financial advisor, broker, or investment company. All financial decisions remain solely with you. For personalized financial advice, we can connect you with licensed professionals.

Technical Support

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

First, make sure you have the latest version of the app. Try closing and reopening the app, or restarting your device. Check your internet connection. If problems persist, our comprehensive support system will be available soon to help with technical issues.

I can't connect my bank account. What's wrong?

Ensure you're using the correct login credentials for your bank. Some financial institutions require additional security steps. Check if your bank is supported in our institution list. If you continue having issues, our support team can provide specific guidance for your financial institution.

How do I contact customer support?

We're building a comprehensive customer support system that will be available soon. In the meantime, you can share feedback through our feedback system (coming soon) or check our Help Centre for additional guidance.
