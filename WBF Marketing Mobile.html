Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how Wellbeing Financial works

How is this different from my bank's app?

Your bank's app only shows you their products. Our AI analyzes the entire market to find you better rates and terms across all lenders, potentially saving you thousands per year.

Is my financial data really safe?

Yes. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption and have read-only access to your accounts. We cannot move money or make purchases. We're SOC 2 compliant and never sell your data.

How do you make money if the app is free?

When you choose to switch to a better financial product we recommend, we may receive a referral fee from the lender. This never affects our recommendations‚Äîwe only succeed when you save money.

What types of financial products do you analyze?

We analyze mortgages, refinancing options, credit cards, personal loans, auto loans, and lines of credit. Our AI continuously monitors rates and terms to find you better deals.

How quickly will I see recommendations?

Initial recommendations appear within minutes of connecting your accounts. Our AI then continuously monitors for new opportunities, sending you alerts when better options become available.

Do I have to switch to the recommended products?

Not at all. We provide recommendations and insights, but all decisions are yours. You can explore options, compare details, and choose what's best for your situation.
