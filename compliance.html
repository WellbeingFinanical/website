Regulatory Compliance

Wellbeing Financial operates in full compliance with Canadian financial regulations and privacy laws, ensuring your protection and our accountability.

Canadian Financial Regulations

As a Canadian fintech company, we operate under comprehensive regulatory oversight:

Provincial Regulations

We comply with provincial financial services regulations across all Canadian provinces where we operate.

Federal Oversight

Our operations align with federal financial regulations and consumer protection laws.

Consumer Protection

We adhere to consumer protection standards that ensure fair and transparent financial services.

Important: Wellbeing Financial is not a deposit-taking institution and is not CDIC insured. We do not provide banking services or hold customer funds.

Privacy Compliance

We maintain strict compliance with Canadian privacy laws:

Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)

  • Transparent collection and use of personal information
  • Consent-based data processing
  • Individual rights to access and correct information
  • Secure handling and storage of personal data
  • Accountability and governance frameworks

Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL)

  • Express consent for commercial electronic messages
  • Clear identification and unsubscribe mechanisms
  • Opt-in marketing communications only
  • Proper record-keeping of consent

Provincial Privacy Laws

We comply with applicable provincial privacy legislation including:

  • Alberta Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA)
  • British Columbia Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA)
  • Quebec Act Respecting the Protection of Personal Information

Financial Advisory Disclosure

Educational Services Only: Wellbeing Financial provides educational technology and information services. We are not licensed as:

  • Financial advisors or investment advisors
  • Insurance brokers or agents
  • Mortgage brokers or agents
  • Credit counselors or debt advisors

All financial advice must be obtained from appropriately licensed professionals.

Third-Party Partnerships

When we connect you with licensed financial professionals:

Mortgage Broker Referrals

  • We only refer to properly licensed mortgage brokers
  • All referral fees are disclosed transparently
  • Brokers operate under provincial licensing requirements
  • Independent advice and suitability assessments

Due Diligence

  • Verification of professional licenses and credentials
  • Regular review of partner compliance status
  • Monitoring of customer feedback and complaints
  • Termination procedures for non-compliant partners

Data Security Compliance

Our security practices meet or exceed industry standards:

Working Toward SOC 2 Type II Compliance

  • Security controls and monitoring
  • Availability and system performance
  • Processing integrity and accuracy
  • Confidentiality of sensitive information
  • Privacy protection and data handling

Financial Industry Standards

  • Bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL/TLS)
  • Multi-factor authentication requirements
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures
