Product Information

What Wellbeing Financial Does

  • Mortgage Analysis: AI-powered evaluation of mortgage rates and refinancing opportunities
  • Credit Card Optimization: Personalized credit card recommendations based on spending patterns
  • Loan Comparisons: Analysis of personal loans, auto loans, and lines of credit
  • Educational Insights: Clear, actionable financial information and opportunity scores

What Makes Us Different

  • AI technology that works 24/7 to monitor financial markets
  • Educational focus rather than sales-driven recommendations
  • Canadian-specific regulatory compliance and market knowledge
  • Bank-level security with read-only access to user accounts
  • Transparent business model with clear disclosure of referral relationships
