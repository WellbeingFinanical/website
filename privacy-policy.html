4. Automated scoring & AI use (agentic framework)

Our Service may generate automated, educational scores (e.g., an "opportunity score" on a 0–900 scale) and comparisons based on your inputs and market data. These outputs help you understand potential opportunities and are not mortgage advice. A licensed mortgage professional must provide suitability advice.

  • We use AI/OCR to extract fields from documents you upload and to assist with data quality checks.
  • We may use AI to summarize or explain comparisons in plain language.

Your options: You can request a human review of any automated assessment or explanation by contacting privacy@wellbeingfinancial.ai. You can choose not to upload documents or not to connect bank accounts (some features may be limited).

5. How we use personal information (purposes)

  • Provide & operate the Service (create account, ingest documents, generate educational comparisons/scores, store preferences).
  • Security & fraud prevention (detect abuse, protect accounts, maintain platform integrity).
  • Product improvement & analytics (understand feature usage, fix bugs, enhance UX).
  • Customer support (respond to requests, troubleshoot).
  • Marketing (optional) (send product updates/offers with your consent; one-click unsubscribe in every message per CASL).
  • Compliance & legal (respond to lawful requests, enforce terms, maintain records).
  • Business continuity (evaluate/complete transactions such as financing, merger or acquisition with appropriate safeguards).

9. Your choices & rights

Under PIPEDA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information and obtain a copy.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete/withdraw consent (we'll explain any features that will stop working).
  • Portability (practical efforts) where feasible.
  • Opt-out of marketing at any time (unsubscribe link / reply "STOP" to SMS).
  • Request human review of automated assessments.

How to exercise: Email privacy@wellbeingfinancial.ai. We respond within 30 days unless a permitted extension is required (we'll notify you).

10. CASL (email/SMS) compliance

We send commercial electronic messages only with express consent (or valid implied consent) and include our identity and a functioning unsubscribe mechanism in every message. You can opt out anytime; service-essential communications (security, legal, account) may still be sent.

11. Security

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Role-based access and least-privilege controls
  • Multi-factor authentication for internal consoles where feasible
  • Network monitoring & logging
  • Vendor due diligence and contractual security obligations
  • Employee confidentiality & training

No method is 100% secure, but we continuously improve our controls. If we discover a breach creating a real risk of significant harm, we will notify affected individuals and regulators as required by law.

12. Children's privacy

The Service is intended for individuals 13+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us to delete it.

13. Cookies & similar technologies

We use cookies, SDKs, and similar technologies for core functionality (e.g., sign-in, session management) and analytics. You may adjust browser or device settings to limit cookies; some features may not function properly without them.

14. Third-party links

The Service may link to third-party sites or services. Their privacy practices are governed by their policies, not this one. Review those policies before providing personal information.

15. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in practices or law. We will post the updated date at the top and provide prominent notice for material changes (e.g., in-app banner or email). Continued use after the effective date means you accept the changes.
