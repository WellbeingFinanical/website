Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how Wellbeing Financial works

How is this different from my bank's app?

Your bank's app only shows you their products. Our AI analyzes options across every lender in Canada, treating each as just another source of capital. We evaluate every financial product purely based on what's best for your situation, regardless of which institution offers it.

Is my financial data really safe?

Yes. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption and have read-only access to your accounts. We cannot move money or make purchases. We're working toward SOC 2 compliance and never sell your data.

How do you make money if the app is free?

When you choose to switch to a financial product we recommend, we may receive a referral fee from the lender. This never affects our recommendations—our only loyalty is to you. We evaluate every product purely based on who offers you the best terms, not who pays us the most.

What types of financial products do you analyze?

We analyze mortgages, refinancing options, credit cards, personal loans, auto loans, and lines of credit. Our AI monitors rates and terms to help identify potential opportunities.

How quickly will I see recommendations?

Initial insights typically appear within minutes of connecting your accounts. Our AI then monitors the market and sends you alerts when it identifies potential opportunities worth exploring.

Do I have to switch to the recommended products?

Not at all. We provide recommendations and insights, but all decisions are yours. You can explore options, compare details, and choose what's best for your situation.

What does "relentlessly pursue your financial wellbeing" mean?

While you're focused on your life, our AI works 24/7 monitoring the entire Canadian financial market. It continuously analyzes thousands of financial products at a volume and frequency that wouldn't be possible to do manually, providing you with the comprehensive financial insights that were once available only to people with personal wealth managers.
